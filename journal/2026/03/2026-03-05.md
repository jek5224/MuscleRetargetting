# Research Journal — 2026-03-05

## DOF Grid Training Pipeline

### Preprocessing & Training
- Preprocessed 9860 samples (10K LHS grid, 140 missing from L_UpLeg resume) across 37 muscles
- PCA k=64 captures 100% variance for all muscles
- Built `train_dof_grid.py` — DistillNetV2 with larger config (hidden=1024, 6 enc / 4 dec res blocks, embed=128)
- Overfit mode: all samples used for training, no validation split, no dropout, no input noise

### Training Tuning
- Initial config: NUM_FREQS=8, LR=5e-4, batch=1024, weight_decay=1e-5
- Changed to overfit-friendly: weight_decay=0, batch=256, LR=1e-3, NUM_FREQS=16, CosineWarmRestarts
- Warm restart at epoch 200 caused loss explosion (1e24) — LR=1e-3 too aggressive
- Fixed: LR→3e-4, added grad clipping (max_norm=1.0), T_mult=2 for doubling restart cycles

### Viewer Integration
- Updated `evaluate.py` and `zygote_mesh_ui.py` for v3_dof inference
- v3_dof uses 7 raw DOFs (no derivatives) as input, predicts all 37 muscles
- Viewer prefers `dof_grid_checkpoints/best.pt` over v2 checkpoint

### Key Issue: Oscillation
- NN predictions follow body tendency but oscillate during motion playback
- Identified NUM_FREQS=16 as potential cause (high-frequency ringing between sparse samples)
- Reduced to NUM_FREQS=6 for next training run
